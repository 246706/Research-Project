Essential Question: What are the characteristics or elements that cause a piece of literature to endure?

Influential works of literature subvert the conventions of, revolutionize, or redefine literature somehow, and are supported by this subversive quality along with the good fortune or literary merit that may have allowed them to gain a significant enough readership to influence. This applies loosely to all art. Of course there are influential works of literature that are considered “so bad that they’re good” or “avant-garde”. There also exist works that have been and continue to be retrospectively evaluated more or less charitably than when they were first received.
